Safety Tips for Women & Children

  • Avoid walking alone at night in deserted areas.
  • Always share your live location with a trusted contact.
  • Keep emergency contacts saved on speed dial.
  • Be aware of your surroundings and trust your instincts.
  • Use personal safety devices like pepper spray.
  • Learn basic self-defense techniques for emergencies.
  • Teach children emergency contact numbers.
